# Bracers of Archery

![Bracers of Archery](bracers-of-archery.png)

While wearing these bracers, you have proficiency with the Longbow and Shortbow, and you gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls made with such weapons.

Notes: Proficiency: Longbow, Proficiency: Shortbow, Damage: Shortbow, Damage: Longbow, Damage, Buff, Combat, Wristwear
