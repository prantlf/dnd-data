# Hat of Disguise

![Hat of Disguise](hat-of-disguise.png)

Wondrous Item, uncommon (requires attunement)

While wearing this hat, you can cast the Disguise Self spell. The spell ends if the hat is removed.

Notes: Deception, Headwear
